Behind the Lens: Meet Our Visionary

Award-winning director Carleton brings over a decade of technical mastery and emotion-driven narrative to every project. Whether crafting music videos, commercials, documentaries or feature films, he elicits authentic human moments on both sides of the camera. Carleton cut his teeth directing shorts and theatrical productions before expanding his directorial scope across mediums, earning acclaim while retaining his signature style of revealing raw humanity through visuals. At Tru Luv Ent, there is a shared passion for cinematic storytelling. For any creative concept, a meticulous production process and wealth of award-winning expertise transforms abstract ideas into visually stunning films and videos that uncover the genuine stories within all people.

He approaches each project with passion to capture compelling visual stories. Carleton leads a creative team to craft videos uniquely tailored for every client.
